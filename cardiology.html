Welcome to Cardiology

At Serandib Hospitals, Our Cardiology Unit is a state-of-the-art facility dedicated to providing comprehensive care for heart and vascular conditions. Equipped with advanced diagnostic and treatment technologies, our team of experienced cardiologists, nurses, and support staff deliver personalized care to patients with a wide range of cardiovascular disorders. From preventive screenings to complex procedures, we focus on ensuring the best outcomes and promoting long-term heart health. Our unit offers services including diagnostic testing, interventional cardiology, and ongoing management of chronic heart conditions, ensuring that every patient receives expert care tailored to their needs
