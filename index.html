About

Welcome to Serandib Hospitals, a leading healthcare facility dedicated to providing exceptional medical care to our community. Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by a team of highly skilled doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals who are committed to delivering compassionate, personalized care. We offer a comprehensive range of services, including emergency care, surgical procedures, diagnostic imaging, maternity services, and specialized treatments. At Serandib Hospitals, we prioritize your health and well-being, ensuring a safe and supportive environment for all our patients. Your health is our mission, and we are here to support you every step of the way.

Services

AtSerandib Hospitals, we offer a wide range of healthcare services designed to meet the diverse needs of our patients. Our services include primary care, specialized treatments, and advanced surgical procedures, all delivered by expert medical professionals. Key services include:

  • Emergency Care: 24/7 emergency services for urgent and life-threatening conditions.
  • Surgical Services: Advanced surgeries, including minimally invasive procedures.
  • Maternity & Childcare: Comprehensive prenatal, delivery, and pediatric care.
  • Specialty Care: Cardiology, orthopedics, neurology, and more.

Pharmacy

The Pharmacy at Serandib Hospitals is committed to providing convenient, safe, and efficient medication services for our patients. Our pharmacy is staffed by licensed pharmacists and skilled technicians who work closely with your healthcare team to ensure the proper management of medications. We offer a full range of prescription and over-the-counter medications, along with expert advice on usage and potential side effects. Whether you're in the hospital or visiting as an outpatient, our pharmacy is here to support your recovery and overall health with prompt, personalized service.
